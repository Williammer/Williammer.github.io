Open Source Projects

Hubot

Hubot is a chat robot for most popular chat networks(gchat,campfire,hipchat,etc). A hubot can do all sorts of neat things and has a decent amount of community contributed scripts.

Cinderella

A Rubygem that sets up a new Mac OSX system for open source development with Chef. It's how I setup my machines and a bunch of other people seem to like it too.

Camo

A simple SSL proxy for images. Useful for making insecure images embedded in websites appear to come from a secure resource.
